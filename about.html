ABOUT THE CLUB

Discover RUET GIS Club

The RUET GIS Club is a vibrant community dedicated to promoting Geographic Information Systems (GIS) awareness, education, and innovation at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET). Since our inception, we've fostered a collaborative environment where students explore the exciting world of GIS and its applications across fields.

We believe in the power of geospatial technology to address real-world challenges and make a positive impact on society. Our mission is to equip students with the knowledge and skills to leverage GIS tools effectively, empowering them to become future leaders in the field - whether you're a seasoned GIS enthusiast or just beginning your journey.

Our mission and objectives

Five commitments guide everything we run.

Promoting GIS Awareness

Raising awareness about the importance and applications of GIS technology among students and the wider community.

Fostering Collaboration & Networking

Opportunities to collaborate on GIS projects, share ideas, and connect with professionals in the field.

Encouraging Innovation & Research

Exploring innovative GIS applications and research projects that address real-world challenges.

Supporting Professional Development

Guest lectures, career panels, and networking events that open doors to GIS-related careers.

Engaging in Community Service

Using GIS to serve our local community — addressing environmental, social, and humanitarian issues.

What we run

Activities - Initiatives

Workshops

Our workshops cover a wide range of topics within GIS - software tutorials, practical applications, and emerging trends. Led by experienced professionals and enthusiasts, they offer hands-on learning for members at every skill level.

Competitions

Exciting GIS competitions that challenge participants to showcase creativity, problem-solving, and GIS expertise — from map-making contests to data analysis challenges — encouraging collaboration and friendly rivalry among members.
